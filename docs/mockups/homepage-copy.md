```
04
The Peoples
Six peoples. One world to outlive.

The Master begins your story with a question, not a menu. You open your eyes. The world sharpens around you. What do you see when you look at your hands?

01 / 06
Draethar

“The warmth radiating from dense, powerful hands.”

Forge-warm. Heavy-handed. Slow to anger, slower to leave.

Mountain-folk and smiths. Their voices carry the way bell-iron carries.

02 / 06
Elari

“Long, fine fingers that tingle with awareness of something beyond the visible.”

Fine-boned. Vein-bright. Aware of what the air conceals.

Once the keepers of the highest libraries. Long memories. Longer grudges.

03 / 06
Korath

“Broad hands, skin with a faint mineral sheen, solid as the stone beneath you.”

Stone-skinned. Quiet. Older than most of what they remember.

From the deep holds beneath Ashen Reach. They speak when they have something to say.

04 / 06
Vaelti

“Quick, nimble hands, every nerve alive to the air currents around them.”

Quick. Wind-attuned. Hear the rumor before the wind arrives.

Steppe-riders and scouts. The Hollow has tried to take them more than once.

05 / 06
Thessyn

“Hands that feel… adaptable, as though they could become anything given time.”

Adaptable. Borrowed-shaped. Become what is needed.

Few in number, never twice the same. The gods are politely confused by them.

06 / 06
Human

“Steady, capable hands — unremarkable except for the determination behind them.”

Unremarkable, except for what is behind it.

Outnumbering, outliving, outlasting. Tending the fires the world keeps trying to put out.

## 08 Questions — What you'll probably ask first.

```
08
Questions
What you'll probably ask first.

The Master answers in character, but here are the short versions.

01
What do I need to play?
−

A phone and a pair of headphones. The phone is a glanceable HUD; everything else happens out loud.

02
Can I play solo, or do I need a group?
+

Both. Solo with your AI companion and the Master, or with a party of up to four — real friends, real voices, in the same scene.

03
I'm not great at improv. Will I freeze?
+

The Master gives you space and follows your lead. Say one word or fifty; the world fills in around you. You'll find your voice in the first hour.

04
How long is a session?
+

Anywhere from five minutes to ninety. The Master scales the scene to the time you have. Five-minute stops by the campfire count.

05
Will the Master remember me?
+

Yes. Your name, your patron, the price you paid the smith three nights ago, the thing your companion said in the rain. The world keeps notes on you between sessions.

06
How is this different from an AI chatbot?
+

A chatbot improvises a scene. The Master runs a world — with rules, with consequences, with continuity. There are dice in this. There is a wound at the center.

07
Can I play while doing other things?
+

That is most of what this game is for. Walks. Commutes. Long quiet hours. Audio-first means your eyes are free for the dishes.

08
When does it launch?
+

Closed playtest waves through 2026, with broader access through 2027. Drop your email below and we'll send a Veil-key when your cohort opens.
