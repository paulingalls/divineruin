# Divine Ruin — Missing AI Generation Prompts

This document contains generation prompts for all audio assets identified as missing or underdeveloped in the main Audio Design Document. Prompts are written to match the style and specificity of the existing prompts and are ready to drop into the relevant sections of the Audio Design Document.

## Combat Sound Effects

### CMB-002 — Sword Strike, Metal on Flesh

**AI Generation Prompt — Sword Strike, Metal on Flesh:**
> Sound effect of a bladed weapon cutting into soft tissue. Duller and wetter than metal-on-metal contact — the sharp ring is absent, replaced by a dense, short thud with a brief fleshy quality. The transient is quick and definitive; no metallic ring follows. The impact should feel physical and final without being gratuitously gory — this is information, not spectacle. Duration: 0.5-1 second. Generate 3 variations with slightly different strike angles and depths.

---

### CMB-003 — Blunt Weapon Impact

**AI Generation Prompt — Blunt Weapon Impact:**
> Sound effect of a heavy blunt weapon — mace, hammer, or war club — making solid contact. The dominant character is low-end: a thick, bassy thud with significant physical weight. The initial transient is rounded rather than sharp, like a dense mass transferring momentum. A brief woody or metallic ring follows depending on the weapon head material. No sharp crack — this is weight and mass, not edge. Duration: 0.5-1 second. Generate 3 variations ranging from glancing impact to full-weight strike.

---

### CMB-004 — Arrow Release and Flight

**AI Generation Prompt — Arrow Release and Flight:**
> Sound of a bow being drawn, released, and an arrow traveling through air. The sequence: a subtle creak of drawn bowstring (0.1s), then a sharp snap-and-hiss as the string releases (0.1s), followed immediately by the whistle and doppler of an arrow in flight — starting bright and close, receding quickly as the arrow travels away. The flight whistle should have a slight pitch shift, suggesting the arrow passing nearby. Total duration: 1-1.5 seconds as a single combined sound. Generate 3 variations with slightly different bow weights and flight tones.

---

### CMB-005 — Arrow Impact, Flesh

**AI Generation Prompt — Arrow Impact, Flesh:**
> Sound of an arrow striking and embedding in a body. A sharp, dense thud — slightly different character from a sword impact: quicker initial transient, less resonance, the brief sound of shaft vibration as the arrow stills. Not wet or dramatic — precise and clinical. Duration: 0.3 seconds. Generate 3 variations.

### CMB-006 — Spell Cast, Fire

**AI Generation Prompt — Spell Cast, Fire:**
> Sound of fire magic being conjured and released. The sequence builds: a soft whoosh of air being drawn inward (0.3s) as energy concentrates, then ignition — a sharp crack followed immediately by a roar of expanding flame. The roar is brief and directional, fading to a dissipating crackle. The whole effect should feel like ignition, not a sustained burn — explosive release, not a campfire. Duration: 1-2 seconds. Generate 3 variations with different intensities (cantrip, mid-tier, powerful).

### CMB-007 — Spell Cast, Ice

**AI Generation Prompt — Spell Cast, Ice:**
> Sound of ice magic crystallizing and releasing. Begins with a high, glassy hiss as moisture in the air freezes (0.2s), then a sharp crystalline crack as the spell releases — like safety glass shattering but with a richer harmonic content. Followed by a brief brittle scatter of ice fragments. The dominant tones are high and bright — this magic is cold and precise, not massive. Duration: 1-2 seconds. Generate 3 variations with slightly different crack qualities.

### CMB-008 — Spell Cast, Force/Arcane

**AI Generation Prompt — Spell Cast, Force/Arcane:**
> Sound of pure force or arcane energy being discharged. Begins as a low subsonic hum building rapidly in pitch and intensity (0.5s) — the sound of energy concentrating — then releases as a concussive, low-frequency thump: felt in the chest, no sharp crack. A brief whoosh of displaced air follows. The dominant character is low-mid frequency, heavy and substantial. This magic doesn't crackle or hiss — it pushes. Duration: 1-2 seconds. Generate 3 variations with different charge durations and release weights.

### CMB-009 — Spell Cast, Healing

**AI Generation Prompt — Spell Cast, Healing:**
> Sound of healing magic activating. Soft and warm — the antithesis of combat sounds. A gentle rising tone, like a quiet choir note appearing from silence, builds briefly (0.5s) then releases as a warm chime with a resonant tail. High-mid frequencies, no bass weight. The emotional quality is relief: this sound is not powerful, it is kind. A very subtle shimmer of harmonics in the decay. Duration: 1-2 seconds. Generate 3 variations with slightly different tonal centers.

### CMB-010 — Player Hit Taken, Light

**AI Generation Prompt — Player Hit Taken, Light:**
> Sound of a character taking a light hit — a glancing blow or minor wound. A dull impact thud (the physical hit) paired simultaneously with a short, low grunt of exertion — not a cry of pain, a reflex response, as if someone absorbing a hit they expected. The grunt is brief and controlled: 0.2 seconds. The impact thud has some body to it but decays quickly. Combined duration: 0.3-0.5 seconds. Generate 3 variations with slightly different exertion qualities (strained, sharp, contained).

### CMB-011 — Player Hit Taken, Heavy

**AI Generation Prompt — Player Hit Taken, Heavy:**
> Sound of a character absorbing a serious, damaging blow. Heavier initial impact — more bass, more mass — followed by a slightly longer recovery sound: a sustained exhale with pain in it, not a scream, more like someone winded and hurt but still standing. The impact transient is denser and longer than the light hit. The emotional quality is vulnerability — this was bad. Duration: 0.5-1 second. Generate 3 variations.

### CMB-012 — Critical Hit Landed

**AI Generation Prompt — Critical Hit Landed (already in doc — prompt missing):**
> Sound effect of landing a perfect, decisive strike. Sharper and more satisfying than a standard sword impact — the transient is crisper, the metallic ring brighter. On the ring's decay, a subtle harmonic overtone emerges: a major third above the fundamental, warm and resonant, suggesting both force and precision. The player should physically feel something rewarding when they hear this. Duration: 0.5-1 second. Generate 3 variations. Note: the harmonic overtone is the key distinguishing feature — it should be present but subtle, not a musical note added on top, rather an acoustic quality of the impact itself.

### CMB-013 — Near Miss

Duration: 0.3-0.5 seconds. Generate 3 variations with slightly different weapon sizes and distances.

### CMB-015 through CMB-018 — Status Effects

**AI Generation Prompt — Status: Poisoned (CMB-015):**

> A brief status-effect sound for stun. A sharp, high-pitched ringing tone — tinnitus-adjacent, as if the character's ears are ringing. Bright transient followed by a quick decay ring. The player should instinctively understand "disorientation." Duration: 1 second. 1 version.

**AI Generation Prompt — Status: Blessed (CMB-017):**
> A brief status-effect sound for a divine blessing being applied. A warm, ascending two-note chime — resonant and clean, slightly cathedral-like. Higher in register, with a golden tonal quality. The emotional content: something good is working in your favor. Duration: 1 second. 1 version.

**AI Generation Prompt — Status: Cursed (CMB-018):**
> A brief status-effect sound for a curse being applied. A discordant interval — two tones that clash (a tritone or minor second), with a slow vibrato that makes them feel unstable and alive. The tone doesn't decay cleanly — it wavers and fades unevenly, as if resisting resolution. The player should feel something unwanted settling in. Duration: 2 seconds. 1 version.

### CMB-019 — Enemy: Undead Signature

**AI Generation Prompt — Enemy Undead Signature:**
> A sound signature for undead enemies — their presence, movement, and character. Bone scraping across stone with slight irregularity (not a clean slide — catches and drags). A dry, hollow rattle like small bones or teeth — not musical, percussive and irregular. Underneath, a low hollow groan — not a voice, more a resonance from a chest cavity that no longer needs to breathe. These three elements can appear separately or together. Duration: 2-4 seconds each. Generate 3 variations: movement (scrape + rattle), presence (groan), and combined.

### CMB-020 — Enemy: Hollow Creature

**AI Generation Prompt — Enemy Hollow Creature Signature:**
> A sound signature for creatures corrupted by the Hollow — things that were once alive and are no longer entirely. Reversed breathing: the inhale sounds like an exhale, the exhale sounds like an inhale — the breathing pattern of something running in the wrong temporal direction. High-frequency clicking at irregular intervals, slightly too fast for insect-like sounds — mechanical without being mechanical, organic without being organic. A wet quality from something that should be dry — not gore, more as if the creature exists in a medium that isn't air. These three elements should feel like they could come from the same source but shouldn't be able to. Duration: 2-4 seconds each. Generate 3 variations: movement, proximity alert, and attack wind-up.

### CMB-021 — Combat Start Sting

**AI Generation Prompt — Combat Start Sting:**
> A 2-second musical stinger that signals combat has begun. A sharp, single percussive hit — frame drum or low tom — with a hard transient and immediate decay. Immediately followed by a tense string accent: two or three staccato notes in a minor second or tritone interval, brief and unresolved. The combined effect is a punctuation mark, not a fanfare: this says "now" not "glory." The unresolved harmonic should leave the listener in a state of readiness. Duration: 2 seconds. 1 version.

---

### CMB-022 — Combat End Sting

**AI Generation Prompt — Combat End Sting:**
> A 2-second musical stinger that signals combat has ended. A short descending melodic phrase — two or three notes moving downward to a resolution — played on cello or low strings. The final note is a stable major or minor tonic: whatever key, it lands and settles. Underneath, the sound of a long, slow exhale — not recorded breath, more the musical equivalent of one: the phrase itself should feel like the releasing of held tension. Warm rather than triumphant. Duration: 2 seconds. 1 version.

## Music Stems

### MUS-002 — Tension

**AI Generation Prompt — Tension Theme:**
> Underscore music for a moment of building danger or uncertainty in a fantasy RPG. The exploration melody, if it was present, has fragmented — only isolated notes remain, separated by silence. A quiet rhythmic pulse appears underneath: low strings playing a single repeated note (col legno or very dry pizzicato), irregular rather than metronomic — the rhythm of a held breath. Dissonant intervals creep into the upper strings: a minor second sustained quietly, unresolved. No percussion kit. No melody. The emotional quality is anticipation with dread underneath — something is about to happen and the player doesn't know if they're ready. Acoustic instruments only. Duration: 60 seconds, seamless loop. The loop should feel like the tension is continuous, not restarting.

---

### MUS-004 — Combat Boss

**AI Generation Prompt — Combat Music, Boss Encounter:**
> Intensified combat music for a high-stakes boss encounter. Builds on the standard combat palette but fuller and more relentless. Full percussion: frame drums, multiple toms, underlying timpani providing gravity. Strings are aggressive and layered — multiple parts in counterpoint, not a single melody. A low brass-like texture (could be low strings or horn-adjacent sounds) provides harmonic weight. The main theme from the standard combat appears in fragments but accelerated and in a darker key. The arrangement is more complex — parts drop in and out, creating the sense of a long, multi-phase encounter. Reserved intensity: the music is powerful but not at maximum — it's holding something back for a phase transition. Acoustic instruments only. Duration: 90 seconds, seamless loop.

---

### MUS-006 — Sorrow

**AI Generation Prompt — Sorrow Theme:**
> A quiet piece for moments of death, loss, and the weight of consequences. Solo cello, playing a simple melodic line in a minor key — not a folk tune, not a theme, just a quiet statement of grief. The melody moves slowly, with long notes and natural pauses between phrases. No accompaniment. No percussion. No harmony. Just the cello and silence. The bow pressure should be gentle — not forced, not vibrato-heavy — just the instrument speaking as plainly as possible. Space between notes is as important as the notes themselves: the silences are not empty, they are the melody continuing in a different register. Duration: 60 seconds, seamless loop. The loop should feel like grief that doesn't end, not a song that restarts.

---

### MUS-008 — Title / Main Menu

**AI Generation Prompt — Title Theme / Main Menu:**
> The identity theme for a dark fantasy audio-first RPG. Opens with the solo cello playing a simple, searching melody — the same melodic character as the exploration theme, but slower, more considered, as if the world is being introduced rather than explored (0-30 seconds). The melody is beautiful but carries the weight of the world's history: something is wrong in this place, and the music knows it, even as it finds the beauty. At 30 seconds, other instruments gradually join: a sustained viola drone, then a distant wordless vocal — high, single voice, barely present. The arrangement fills gently, never swelling aggressively — it builds the way light fills a room at dawn. Around 90 seconds, the full acoustic ensemble is present: strings, a low whistle, the vocal, quiet hand percussion underneath. Then the arrangement begins to recede, back toward the solo cello, and the loop returns to its opening. The emotional message: this is a world worth caring about, and something has gone wrong in it, and you are the person who might help. Duration: 120 seconds, seamless loop. Acoustic instruments only — no synthesizers, no modern production. This is the player's first impression of the game world; it should feel like a place, not a product.

## Stingers

### STG-001 — Quest Stage Complete

**AI Generation Prompt — Quest Stage Complete Stinger:**
> A short musical phrase signaling meaningful progress. 3-4 notes ascending in a bright major key, played on upper strings or a warm plucked instrument — the character of a small resolution, a step forward. Not a fanfare — more like a quiet acknowledgment. The final note settles confidently and rings briefly. Duration: 3-5 seconds. 1 version. This stinger will play frequently; it should be satisfying but not demanding of attention.

---

### STG-002 — Quest Complete

**AI Generation Prompt — Quest Complete Stinger:**
> A fuller version of the stage complete stinger for full quest resolution. The same ascending melodic character, but harmonized — two or three voices reaching the resolution together. A brief swell before the final chord, and the final chord sustains longer with a warm decay. More instruments: the plucked or string lead plus a cello underneath providing the harmonic foundation. The feeling: this chapter is closed. Duration: 5-7 seconds. 1 version.

---

### STG-004 — Level Up

**AI Generation Prompt — Level Up Stinger:**
> A warm, ascending musical phrase for character growth. Begins low and opens upward — a melodic gesture of expansion, not triumph. The instrumentation has breadth: strings in the mid-register joined by a sustained high woodwind note at the peak. The harmonic quality is open — major, spacious, neither intimate nor epic. The player should feel like they've grown into something larger. Not a celebration — an acknowledgment. Duration: 5-7 seconds. 1 version.

---

### STG-005 — Faction Reputation Shift

**AI Generation Prompt — Faction Reputation Shift Stinger:**
> A neutral musical marker for a change in standing — could be positive or negative. A single sustained note that bends slightly in pitch — not a melody, an inflection. Followed by a short, ambiguous two-note phrase: a major second or a minor third that resolves to neither major nor minor stability. The emotional message is "something shifted" not "something good happened" or "something bad happened." Duration: 2-3 seconds. 1 version.

### STG-006 — God Whisper Arrival

**AI Generation Prompt — God Whisper Arrival Stinger:**
> A 3-5 second audio moment that signals a god is about to speak — a presence arriving in the audio field. The sound varies by deity (see variants below), but all share a core quality: a sense of scale that transcends the normal game world. The player should feel that the space around them has changed before the god speaks. Base structure: a subsonic resonance appearing (felt before heard), followed by a brief textural element specific to the deity, then a brief moment of silence before the god's voice begins.

> **Kaelen variant:** A low iron resonance, like a massive bell struck once and left to ring — deep, stone-hall echo. Dry and massive.
>
> **Syrath variant:** A subtle stereo shift — the ambient audio moves slightly between ears, becoming briefly disorienting. Then close silence, intimate and pressured, as if the air itself has leaned in.
>
> **Veythar variant:** A crystalline harmonic shimmer — high, glassy tones suggesting vast stored knowledge. Like tuning forks of different sizes resonating simultaneously.
>
> **Aelora variant:** A warm, low swell — like a fire finding new fuel. Substantial and encompassing, the acoustic equivalent of warmth.
>
> **Mortaen variant:** A sound that seems to slow down the audio around it — other ambient sounds subtly stretch and lower slightly in pitch as this tone arrives. Quiet, absolute, outside of time.

Duration: 3-5 seconds each. Generate 1 per deity for MVP (Kaelen, Syrath, Veythar, Aelora, Mortaen).

---

### STG-007 — Death

**AI Generation Prompt — Death Stinger:**
> A solemn audio moment for player or companion death. Not dramatic, not horror — this is the sound of the world going quiet. The ambient audio (wherever it is playing) begins a slow fade to near-silence. Over this fade, a single cello note — low, sustained, played with the bow moving very slowly and with minimal pressure, as if the sound itself is exhausted. The note holds for 3-4 seconds, then fades without resolution. No chord. No harmonics. Just the note, alone, and then nothing. Duration: 5-7 seconds. This is not a loop — it plays once and ends in silence.

### STG-008 — Session Start

**AI Generation Prompt — Session Start Stinger:**
> The sound of entering the game world. Begins with a brief, quiet moment — near silence, just the faintest low resonance, like a held breath (0.5s). Then a soft rush of air, as if a door has opened — not a whoosh, more like the feeling of a space changing its pressure (0.5s). Immediately followed by the first note of the ambient soundscape for the current location: the world arriving, not being switched on. Underneath, a single warm cello note rises briefly and settles into the ambient bed. The transition from silence to world should feel like waking up in a place you know. Duration: 3-5 seconds total, designed to blend seamlessly into whatever ambient soundscape follows.

### STG-009 — Session End

**AI Generation Prompt — Session End Stinger:**
> The sound of the world gently releasing the player. The ambient soundscape (wherever it is) begins a slow, graceful fade. Over this fade, a single final note from the exploration theme plays — the cello, unhurried, like a last word. A very quiet distant chime, almost inaudible, marks the moment the world goes fully silent. Then silence — warm, not empty — as if the world is still there, waiting. Duration: 5-7 seconds. This is not a loop — it plays once and ends in stillness.

## Sable's Sound Palette

Sable is the silent companion — no voice, only sounds. These clips are her "vocabulary" and the player will learn to read them. Each should feel animal but not directly mapped to any real species — something between feline, corvid, and something unnamed.

**AI Generation Prompt — Sable: Content / At Rest:**
> A soft, low, intermittent trill — the vocal equivalent of contentment from an animal that doesn't quite purr. Short, rhythmic, produced in the throat rather than the chest. Warm tonal quality, moderate pitch. The sound of something at ease in the present moment. Duration: 1-2 seconds per trill, with natural pause between. Generate 3 variations.

**AI Generation Prompt — Sable: Curious / Interested:**
> A brief, rising chirp — two notes, ascending, questioning. The sound of attention directed at something unknown. Lighter than the content trill, produced higher in the vocal range. Quick and clean, like a small punctuation mark of interest. Duration: 0.5-1 second. Generate 4 variations with slightly different intervals and intensities.

**AI Generation Prompt — Sable: Alert / Alarmed:**
> A short, sharp bark-like call — not a dog bark, more like a corvid alarm call but softer, with a vocal quality. Single syllable, hard transient, immediate decay. The sound of something that has identified a threat and is signaling it clearly. Urgent without being panicked. Duration: 0.3-0.5 seconds. Generate 3 variations (mild alert, strong alert, urgent alarm).

**AI Generation Prompt — Sable: Distressed / In Pain:**
> A keening whine — sustained, with a slight wavering pitch. The sound of distress from an animal that doesn't cry: vocal, vulnerable, hard to listen to without wanting to help. Not a scream, not a howl — a sustained appeal. Mid-register, with the quality of something endured rather than released. Duration: 2-3 seconds. Generate 2 variations (mild distress, significant pain).

**AI Generation Prompt — Sable: Affectionate / Bonded:**
> A low, soft sound used specifically when Sable is near the player and at ease with them — something between a hum and a murmur, more sustained than the content trill, quieter and more intimate. The sound of trust, expressed without language. Duration: 2-4 seconds. Generate 2 variations.

### ENV-004 — Millhaven Village, Daytime

**AI Generation Prompt — Millhaven Village, Daytime:**

### ENV-008 — Wilderness Road, Night (Without Corruption)

**AI Generation Prompt — Forest Road, Night, Normal:**
> Ambient soundscape for a forest road at night with no supernatural element — just the natural world after dark. Foundation of night wind through trees — more prominent than daytime wind, the canopy moving more freely. Layered with: dense nocturnal insect sounds (crickets, other night insects at the edge of perception), an owl call in the middle distance (tawny owl — two-syllable call, then silence, then again), the occasional rustle of small creatures in underbrush, the crack of a branch somewhere in the dark that could be a deer. Unlike the corrupted night forest, the sounds are continuous and natural — no silences, no wrong notes. The darkness is simply darkness: inhabited, normal, slightly wild. Mood: the comfortable unease of being outside at night in a place that belongs to nocturnal things, not you. 60-second seamless loop.
